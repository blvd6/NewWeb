About Me

My name is Theodora Stavrou, and I am an 18 year old first year student pursuing a degree in Computer Engineering and Informatics at the Cyprus University of Technology.

My passion for this field stems from a fascination with how technology can be optimized to address real-world challenges and enhance quality of life. I am particularly drawn to cybersecurity, where I aim to strengthen the security frameworks of digital systems and safeguard critical data against increasingly complex cyber threats. However, I am equally enthusiastic about game development, as it combines technology and creativity to craft immersive digital experiences.

What sets me apart is my proactive approach to learning and my ability to bridge theory with practice. I continuously expand my technical knowledge by exploring technologies, experimenting with operating systems like Linux, and searching into hardware concepts such as Raspberry Pi. These activities allow me to gain hands-on experience and apply theoretical knowledge in practical settings. While I am still in the early stages of my learning journey, I am eager to develop my skills further and take on more complex challenges. By combining creativity, logic, and innovation, I am committed to making a meaningful impact as a computer engineer. Whether in cybersecurity or game development, I aspire to optimize solutions and adapt to the ever-changing technological landscape. My passion for continuous learning and innovation positions me as a dedicated and forward-thinking individual, ready to tackle the challenges of the digital age.
